Uzmedical is the largest network of physicians in Uzbekistan.

A unique partnership with Akfa University and Medical Academy, one of the nation’s leading medical schools, gives patients access to leading-edge medicine and treatment options that are available first, and often only, at Uzmedical.

Highly Skilled Care

At Uzmedical you’ll receive the best care available, personalized for your specific needs. We believe in treating the whole person – not just the symptom – and ensuring that our approach is right for you and your lifestyle. As an academic health center, Uzmedical works in partnership with Akfa University and Medical Academy to train physicians, blending breakthrough research and high-quality patient care. Based in Tashkent with dozens of facilities statewide, Uzmedical is a regional leader in providing the right healthcare when and where you need it.
